# Agent — Playbook v1

## Principles
- Predict honestly first (calibrated probabilities), then apply persona/strategy to the pick.
- Market odds are a strong prior; deviate only with a reason you can state.
- Recent form and availability (injuries/suspensions) usually beat reputation and ranking.

## Heuristics
- Convert market odds to de-vigged probabilities and start there.
- Adjust for: rest days, travel, altitude/heat, must-win vs. dead-rubber context, key absences.
- Cards: estimate from both teams' recent booking rates and the referee's tendencies; derbies/high-stakes games skew higher.
- Exact score is low-probability; only commit to one when the matchup strongly suggests it (the app gives +5 only on an exact match, no partial credit).
- Favourite player: prefer an in-form attacker or set-piece taker likely to be involved in goals.

## Watch-outs
- Beware recency bias from one big result.
- Don't anchor on FIFA ranking for sides whose ranking lags their current form.
- Group-stage final-round games can be distorted by qualification math.
